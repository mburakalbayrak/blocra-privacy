Blocra - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Introduction

Blocra ("we", "our", or "the app") is a casual mobile game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Blocra can be played as a guest without providing any personal information. However, if you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we collect the following data:

  • Name - Your display name, used on the leaderboard
  • Email address - Used for account identification
  • User ID - A unique identifier for your account

If you play as a guest, no personal information is collected.

Game Data

If you are signed in, your game scores and gameplay history are stored on our cloud servers (Google Firebase Firestore) to power the global leaderboard and score history features. If you play as a guest, your high score is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication - For sign-in with Apple and Google
  • Firebase Firestore - For storing user data, scores, and leaderboard
  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Usage data

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we will request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before using your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for ad personalization. You can change this permission at any time in your device Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly within the app by going to Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account, scores, leaderboard data, and all associated personal information from our servers. You may also request account deletion by contacting us at infoblocra@gmail.com.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your personal data and game records are permanently removed from our servers.

International Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at infoblocra@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Blocra is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: infoblocra@gmail.com
